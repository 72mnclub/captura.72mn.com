WHERE THE NAME COMES FROM 这个名字怎么来的

The notch is humanity's first note. 人类最早的笔记,是一条刻痕。

12,000 years before written language, we kept track by carving notches on tally sticks. Notch is that ancient act, rebuilt for voice and AI — so the things you don't want to forget actually stay. 一万两千年前还没有文字,人类就在木棒上刻 notch 来记账。Notch 把这个古老的动作重做了一遍 — 用语音、用 AI,让你不想忘的事真的留得住。
