Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Notch is built local-first. Your notes live on your phone as plain files you own. We only send the slice of data each AI request needs — never your whole library, never to third-party advertisers, never for our training.

1. What we collect

1.1 Data that stays on your device

  • Your notes — every notch you make is stored locally in your phone's file system (visible in the iOS Files app, inside your Vault). They never leave your device unless you explicitly export, share, or trigger an AI action on them.
  • Voice recordings — when you record, the audio is transcribed on-device using Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer. The audio file is discarded after transcription unless you choose to keep it.

1.2 Data we send to AI providers

When you ask Notch's AI to clean, weave, discuss, or reflect, we send the relevant slice of your notes to a language-model provider. v1 routes through our Pool (which uses Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, DeepSeek, and xAI Grok depending on the task), or directly to your own provider if you've set up Bring-Your-Own-Token mode.

We do not send any data the request doesn't need. We do not retain copies of what was sent. The providers process the request and discard it per their own retention policies (linked below).

1.3 Account data

When you create an account, we store:

  • Email address (for sign-in)
  • A Notch user ID (random UUID)
  • Subscription state and billing receipts (for Plus subscribers — handled by Apple's StoreKit)
  • Credit balance and usage ledger (for Free users)

We do not store passwords (we use passwordless email sign-in or Sign in with Apple).

1.4 Diagnostics

If the app crashes or hits a bug, anonymized error reports are sent to Sentry (our error-tracking provider). No personal note content is included in these reports.

2. What we don't collect

  • We don't sell your data. Ever.
  • We don't use your notes to train AI models — neither ours nor any provider's.
  • We don't run advertising on Notch.
  • We don't share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics platforms.
  • We don't track you across other apps or websites.

3. Your rights

  • Export — every note is a plain markdown file. AirDrop, copy, sync to iCloud, or zip your entire Vault. You don't need our permission and you don't need our cooperation.
  • Delete — delete a note locally and it's gone (no server-side ghost). Delete your account and we wipe your server-side records within 30 days.
  • Access — your data IS your data. There's no extra "data access request" to file. It's already in your hands.
  • Correct — same as above. Edit any file directly.

4. Third-party services we use

  • Apple Sign-In / StoreKit — auth + subscription billing
  • Supabase — account + entitlement records, deployed in [region]
  • Google Gemini API — AI provider (Pool routing)
  • Anthropic API — AI provider (Pool routing)
  • DeepSeek API — AI provider (Pool routing)
  • xAI API — AI provider (Pool routing)
  • Tavily — web search (only when you explicitly invoke an AI action that needs current information)
  • Sentry — anonymized error tracking

5. Children

Notch is not designed for children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

6. Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date and, for significant changes, notify you in-app or by email.

7. Contact

Questions? Reach us at privacy@notch.72mn.com.
