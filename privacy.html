Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026 · Publisher: 72mn LLC

This Privacy Policy explains how Captura handles information when you use the Captura iOS app, website, subscriptions, AI features, voice features, and related services (together, the "Service").

1. Short version

Captura is designed as a private, local-first capture app.

  • Your chats, memos, notes, and generated outputs are stored on your device.
  • Captura does not provide group chats or shared spaces.
  • Captura does not store your chats or memos in a server-side chat database.
  • If you enable sync, your data may sync through your private Apple iCloud account using CloudKit.
  • If you use AI, transcription, or creation features, the content needed for that request may be sent to Captura servers and AI or transcription providers to process the request.
  • Captura may process account, subscription, credit, entitlement, diagnostic, and security information to operate the app.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your chats, memos, or notes for advertising profiles or cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • We do not intentionally use your personal content to train foundation AI models.

2. Information Captura handles

Content you create

Captura may store or process content you create in the app, such as:

  • Text notes, memos, chats, todos, reminders, labels, comments, reactions, and collections.
  • Voice recordings, transcripts, and waveform metadata.
  • AI prompts, AI replies, summaries, titles, cleaned-up text, and generated outputs.
  • Search queries and local search or semantic-index data.
  • Files or exports that you choose to create, open, import, or share.

This content is stored locally on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, it may also be stored in your private iCloud account through Apple CloudKit. Captura does not store your chats or memos as permanent server-side chat records.

AI and creation requests

When you use AI-powered features, Captura sends only the content needed to complete that request. This may include your prompt, selected recent memos, selected context, generation options, model parameters, and the AI response.

Some AI requests are processed through Captura's server-side AI route so that we can provide included models, entitlement checks, provider routing, safety handling, and usage accounting. Async creation jobs may temporarily store the submitted request and generated result until the app collects the result; the result is then written back to your local device storage and the server job is deleted.

These AI-processing paths are not a server-side chat archive. We do not use them to store your full chat history on Captura servers.

Voice and transcription

If you use voice features, Captura may process microphone audio, transcripts, language hints, file size, duration, and related technical data.

Some transcription may happen on your device through Apple speech-recognition technology. If you use premium or server-backed transcription, audio may be uploaded to Captura and forwarded to a transcription provider to produce a transcript. Captura is designed to process uploaded audio for the request and not keep it as a permanent server-side recording.

Account and sign-in information

If you sign in, subscribe, restore purchases, use Plus features, redeem credits, or use server-backed AI features, Captura may process:

  • Apple sign-in identifiers and authentication session data.
  • Email address if Apple provides it, including private relay addresses if you use Hide My Email.
  • Account ID, display name, avatar choice, app user ID, and entitlement state.
  • Session tokens needed to keep you signed in.

Purchases, subscriptions, and credits

If you subscribe to Captura Plus+, restore a purchase, redeem credits, or use included AI features, Captura may process:

  • App Store product IDs, transaction IDs, original transaction IDs, subscription status, renewal state, current period dates, refund/revocation status, and StoreKit signed transaction information.
  • Credit balance, grants, debits, usage ledger entries, token counts, model/provider metadata, entitlement snapshots, and related billing or support records.

Apple processes App Store payments. Captura does not receive your full payment card details from Apple.

Diagnostics and app operations

Captura may process:

  • App version, OS version, device model, locale, time zone, crash reports, error reports, and performance or reliability diagnostics.
  • Push notification tokens and notification preferences.
  • Feature flags, service health metadata, and abuse-prevention or security logs.

Our diagnostics are designed to avoid collecting personal content. We use scrubbing controls intended to remove prompts, transcripts, message content, API keys, tokens, email addresses, display names, and similar sensitive fields before diagnostic events are sent. No automated scrubber is perfect.

Information Captura does not intentionally collect

Captura does not intentionally collect precise location, contacts, calendars, photos, camera data, health data, or advertising identifiers unless a future feature asks for permission and this policy is updated.

Captura does not offer group chats or shared spaces in the current app.

3. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide, maintain, secure, and improve Captura.
  • Store and display your local content.
  • Sync your content across your Apple devices through iCloud when enabled.
  • Provide AI replies, cleanup, titles, summaries, creations, search, transcription, reminders, and exports.
  • Verify subscriptions, Plus access, credits, entitlements, restores, and refunds.
  • Send notifications or app status messages you enable.
  • Debug crashes, investigate errors, prevent abuse, enforce limits, and protect users.
  • Comply with law, App Store requirements, tax, accounting, and security obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your personal content for cross-context behavioral advertising.

We do not intentionally use your chats, memos, notes, or voice recordings to train foundation AI models. Third-party AI or transcription providers may process requests according to their own terms and privacy policies.

4. Where information is stored

On your device

Captura stores your content on your device, including local app databases and related files. iOS data-protection features help protect local files when your device is locked.

iCloud

If sync is enabled and available, Captura uses Apple CloudKit to sync data through your private iCloud account. Apple handles iCloud under Apple's terms and privacy policies. Captura cannot control all Apple iCloud retention, backup, or account settings.

Captura servers

Captura servers may process account, subscription, entitlement, credit, AI request, creation job, transcription, diagnostic, and security information needed to operate server-backed features.

Captura servers do not store your chats or memos as a permanent server-side chat database.

Third-party providers

Depending on the feature, Captura may use third-party providers for hosting, database infrastructure, error reporting, AI models, transcription, App Store purchases, and related operations. Provider availability and the exact provider list may change over time. We will update this policy or in-app disclosures when material practices change.

5. When information is shared

We may share information:

  • With service providers that help us operate, host, secure, monitor, or support Captura.
  • With Apple for Sign in with Apple, iCloud, App Store purchases, push notifications, and device services.
  • With AI or transcription providers when you use features that require them.
  • With professional advisors, auditors, payment/tax providers, legal authorities, or law enforcement when required or appropriate.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, subject to appropriate protections.
  • With your direction or consent.

We do not sell personal information for money and do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

6. Your choices

You can:

  • Use Captura's local capture features without storing your chats or memos on Captura servers.
  • Manage iCloud sync through your Apple account and iOS settings.
  • Manage microphone, speech recognition, notifications, and cellular-data permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Export supported content from the app.
  • Delete content in the app where controls are provided.
  • Sign out of your account.
  • Request account deletion where available in the app.
  • Manage or cancel App Store subscriptions in your Apple account settings.

If you delete the app, iOS may remove local app data from that device. iCloud-synced data, App Store purchase records, and provider records may not be deleted automatically.

7. Deletion and retention

You may delete content in Captura and may request deletion of your account where the app provides that option. Account deletion is designed to remove server-side account data controlled by Captura, subject to retention needed for legal, tax, accounting, fraud-prevention, security, dispute-resolution, backup, or audit purposes.

Local data on your device remains until you delete it, wipe local data, or uninstall the app. iCloud-synced data may remain in your Apple account until removed through Captura, iCloud controls, or Apple account controls.

AI, transcription, and infrastructure providers may retain information according to their own terms and policies. Captura cannot delete data held independently by Apple or other providers except where those providers support deletion through our account or service controls.

8. Privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, objection, withdrawal of consent, or appeal of a privacy decision.

To make a privacy request, contact us at contact@72mn.com. We may need to verify your request. If you use Sign in with Apple Hide My Email, please contact us from the email address associated with your Captura account or provide enough information to verify account ownership.

California residents may have additional rights under California privacy laws. We do not sell personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. If that changes, we will update this policy and provide required opt-out controls.

Residents of the EEA, UK, Switzerland, and similar jurisdictions may have rights under local data-protection laws. Where required, our legal bases may include performance of a contract, consent, legitimate interests, compliance with legal obligations, and protection of vital or public interests.

9. Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information, including platform security controls, authentication, access controls, transport security, row-level access controls for server records, diagnostic scrubbing, and operational monitoring.

No system is perfectly secure. You are responsible for protecting your device, Apple account, Captura account, and exported files.

10. Children

Captura is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to Captura, contact us at contact@72mn.com.

11. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will provide notice through the app, App Store listing, website, email, or another reasonable method.
